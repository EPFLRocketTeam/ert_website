How to join us

If you’re interested in joining one of our projects or semester projects we recommend being at the recruitement event. You’ll have the opportunity to meet the team leaders and heads of project, which will present the different projects and the workload in greater details.

The Semester projects shown here are related to the research projects (Icarus and Hyperion) as well as our competition project.

We also have a variety of positions open, where all sections of EPFL, UNIL, SAWI, HEC and HEIG-VD are welcome!
